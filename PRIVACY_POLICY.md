# Privacy Policy for ShredCountdown

Ajay Kumar Dhiman ("we," "our," or "us") operates the ShredCountdown mobile application (the "App"). This page informs you of our policies regarding the collection, use, and disclosure of personal data when you use our App and the choices you have associated with that data.

## 1. Information Collection and Use

   While using our App, we may ask you to provide certain personally identifiable information that can be used to contact or identify you, or to calculate fitness metrics.
   Personal Data
   During the onboarding process, you provide physical metrics including:
    • Age, Gender, Height, and Weight.
    • Body measurements (Waist, Neck, and Hips).
    • Activity level.
   This data is used exclusively to calculate your TDEE (Total Daily Energy Expenditure), Body Fat Percentage, and calorie goals.

## 2. Permissions and Device Features

   Our App requires access to certain device features to function:

### Camera

   We use the camera to allow you to take "Day 1" and "Victory" selfies.
   • Privacy Note: Photos taken within the App are stored locally on your device or in the App's private cache. We do not upload these photos to any external servers. You have full 		  control over sharing these images via third-party apps.

### Storage

   The App requires storage access to save and retrieve your transformation photos and progress logs.

### Notifications

   We use notifications to send you daily mission reminders at the time you specify.

## 3. Local Data Storage

   All progress logs, calorie data, and profile information are stored locally on your device using an internal database. We do not maintain a central server, and we do not have access to your personal fitness history.

## 4. Third-Party Service Providers

   We may employ third-party companies and individuals to facilitate our App, specifically for monetization:

### Advertisements (AdMob)

   We use Google AdMob to display advertisements. AdMob may collect and use data (such as your Device Advertising ID) to show personalized or non-personalized ads.
   • Google Privacy & Terms

## 5. Security of Data

   The security of your data is important to us, but remember that no method of transmission over the Internet, or method of electronic storage is 100% secure. While we strive to use commercially acceptable means to protect your Personal Data through local encryption and secure Android standards, we cannot guarantee its absolute security.

## 6. Children's Privacy

   Our App does not address anyone under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personally identifiable information from children under 13. If you are a parent or guardian and you are aware that your child has provided us with Personal Data, please contact us.

## 7. Changes to This Privacy Policy

   We may update our Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new Privacy Policy on this page and updating the "Last Updated" date at the top.
